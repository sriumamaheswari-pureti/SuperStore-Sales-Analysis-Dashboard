# SuperStore Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **SuperStore Performance Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, shipping modes, payment methods, and product categories. It enables business stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify sales trends, and make data-driven decisions through dynamic visualizations and filters.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Businesses generate large volumes of sales data, making it difficult to identify:

* Sales and profit trends over time
* Best-performing product categories and sub-categories
* Customer purchasing behavior
* Preferred payment methods
* Impact of shipping modes on sales
* Regional sales performance

The objective was to build an interactive dashboard that provides a comprehensive overview of business performance.

---

## 🚀 Objectives

* Analyze overall Sales, Profit, and Quantity
* Monitor monthly Sales and Profit trends
* Compare sales across different regions
* Identify top-performing categories and sub-categories
* Analyze customer segments
* Evaluate shipping and payment preferences
* Enable interactive filtering for deeper insights

## 📈 Key Insights

* Total Sales reached **1.57M**.
* Total Profit generated was **175.26K**.
* Over **22K** products were sold.
* **Office Supplies** contributed the highest sales among all categories.
* **Phones** generated the highest sales among sub-categories.
* **Consumer** segment contributed nearly **48%** of total sales.
* **Standard Class** shipping accounted for the majority of orders.
* **Cash on Delivery (COD)** was the most preferred payment method.
* Monthly sales and profits showed seasonal fluctuations, indicating peak and low-demand periods.
